Welcome to my webpage

I am an assistant professor at the Institute of Mathematics of Polish Academy of Sciences (IMPAN), interested in applied and combinatorial algebraic geometry. Previously, I was an FWO postdoctoral fellow at KU Leuven, and before that I was a postdoc at the Mathematical Institute of the University of Bern, where I worked in the research group of Professor Jan Draisma. I did my PhD at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences in Leipzig; my advisor was Mateusz Michałek.
